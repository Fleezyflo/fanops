compile-time graph** (a DAG by construction; the level is well-defined for every module).

**Assertion:**

> **For every MODULE-LEVEL `fanops → fanops` import edge `a → b`: `level(b) < level(a)`.**

> 🔴 **The 56 strictly-upward edges are the ALLOWED-LAZY set. They must STAY lazy.**
> **A module-level edge to an equal-or-higher level is REJECTED.**

**Classify imports by SYNTACTIC POSITION**, exactly as Cycle 5 did:

- module level → **compile-time** *(the constrained set)*
