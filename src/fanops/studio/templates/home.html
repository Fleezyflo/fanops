Get started

How FanOps works — three steps from zero to your first per-account clips. The Next button in the workflow bar above always points to your current step.

  1. Connect an account — link a handle + platform in Go Live.
  2. Make clips from a video — upload footage in Make; it cuts clips + writes captions per account.
  3. Review & approve — each account's clips land in the Review matrix; nothing ships until you approve.

Tip: give each account a persona so its clip length, on-screen hook, and caption differ.
